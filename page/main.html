About Us

Our Story

Megnum is more than just a place to grab a coffee. It's a cozy haven where friends gather, neighbors connect, and the aroma of freshly brewed coffee fills the air. Founded in 2024 by Piyush Dahle, our cafe was born out of a passion for good food.

Our Atmosphere

Step inside our cafe and be greeted by a warm, inviting ambiance. Our cozy seating, soft lighting, and eclectic decor create a perfect setting for relaxation and conversation. Whether you're enjoying a solo coffee or catching up with friends, you'll feel right at home.

Our Food and Drinks

Our menu features a variety of delicious options, from classic coffee drinks to homemade pastries and hearty sandwiches. We take pride in our freshly brewed coffee, using only the finest beans sourced from around the world. Our pastries are baked daily in-house, ensuring a fresh and flavorful taste.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a warm and inviting atmosphere where people can relax, unwind, and enjoy delicious, homemade treats. We believe that every cup of coffee and every bite of food should be a delightful experience.
